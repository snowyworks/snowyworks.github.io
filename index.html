ABOUT

SnowyWorks was founded in 2017 by Drew D. Lenhart and is based in New Haven, Indiana. The company began with its core publication, Future Sci-Fi Tales, a science fiction anthology series featuring stories from independent comic creators. Since then, the company has sky rocketed with new titles, including one-shots such as De-Aged and Calzer's Clock, Bitter Awakening, as well as new ongoing series Caspian Porter and The Monster's Cleanup Guy.
