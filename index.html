SUBMISSIONS




Presented by SnowyWorks LLC & The Indie Comix Dispatch


Submission period: November 20, 2020 to February 20, 2021

Woah, a new contest!? Do you want to get published? Indie Comix Dispatch and SnowyWorks are teaming up! We are putting together a brand new anthology book and we are looking for up and coming creators to submit their short story for inclusion into Tales of the Dispatch. We are looking for FIVE stories for this first fantastic issue!

We will be accepting short sequential art stories, either black and white or full color! We want stories between six to twelve pages, nothing more, nothing less. We are looking for unique stories that blow the minds of readers! We are looking for works that are immersive, character-driven, and most of all, a well-crafted story! Any genre will be accepted!
