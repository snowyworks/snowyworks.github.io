Weston is a successful business man who also happens to be a Lycan. He uses his abilities to further his life to his advantage by any means possible, any means. This all stems from a childhood full of bullying. His childhood bully, also a rival businessman, sends a package that ignites a fury within Weston and sets on a path of revenge...

Written by Drew D. Lenhart
Artwork by Dell Barras
Cover colors by Bea Navarro
