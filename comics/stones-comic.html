When mysterious objects from space change the course of history, how will the human race cope with such a force? Acceptance or destruction? Stones collects the first two stories in a four-story collection.

"Stones" a short story showing through time, how humanity deals with the objects. Are these objects a gift, or a curse?

"Under" follows the story of a farmer as he discovers something mysterious under the fallen object on his farm. Will he discover what he's looking for, or lose himself in the process?

Beautiful artwork by Alberto Pessoa and written by Drew D. Lenhart
