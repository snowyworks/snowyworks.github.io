Issue #04

Tales from the future! Future Sci-Fi Tales anthology series issue #4 features five exciting tales of science fiction. Features bullies in space, trouble with police issued applications, a martian hypochondriac and an apocalyptic tale of Big Pharma!

Stories are written by Drew D. Lenhart, Julio Paz and Matt Kund. Fantastic and astounding illustrations by Scott Braren, Cristian Iglesias, LJ Phillips, Noah Ray and Greg Floch.

Stories: In Chet We Trust, Apps War, Dust, RX-tinction and We Travel.

Cover by Juan Fleites.
