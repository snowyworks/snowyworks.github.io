Tales from the future! Read three exciting short science fiction stories featuring surgeons miniaturized to combat a new strain of bacteria, a scientist who performed the impossible to save the love of his life, and a stranded man unexpectedly abducted by aliens.

Stories are written by Julio Paz and Felipi De Souza. Fantastic and astounding illustrations by Alberto Pessoa, Edu Pelayo & Cristian Navarro.

Features: Micromythos, Artificaos Intelligence & The Lovers.

ComixCentral Release: August 12, 2019.
