Isolation and paranoia affect a man living on mars. A group of students gamble precisely when their teacher will die. A robot with a human brain cannot overcome his own compulsive behaviors, even when facing a critical space mission. Three tales, three twisted outcomes...tune in for the Midnyght Tryfecta...

Story & Letters: Drew D. Lenhart
Pencils & Inks: LJ Phillips
