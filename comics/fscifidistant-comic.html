Tales from the future! Plucked from the pages of the classic anthology series by author Drew D. Lenhart. Featuring pesky rodents from another planet, aging space men, robots, slow droids, shapeshifting aliens and more! This book includes 8 black and white stories for the true sci-fi lover.

Story & Letters: Drew D. Lenhart
Illustrations: LJ Phillips, Hernan Molina, Louie Escauriaga, Scott Braren, Rowel Roque, Lee Milewski, and Juan Fleites
