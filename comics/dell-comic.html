Art book

Legendary artist, Dell Barras is a 40 year veteran in the comic book industry having worked with Marvel UK, DC Comics, Antarctic Press and other indie publishers. He spent most of his career as a storyboard artist for Warner Bros., Dreamworks, Nickelodeon, Disney, Fox, Marvel Films, Cartoon Network and Hanna Barbara. This book collects various art pieces throughout his career as well as new work.
