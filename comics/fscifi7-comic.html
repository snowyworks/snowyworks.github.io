Tales from the future! A special new edition of the Future Sci-Fi Tales anthology series features NINE tales of sci-fi.

Read about faring space captains, robots, treason, aliens, crash landings, its all here and more! These stories are sci-fi AND set in the future.

Stories are written by AJ Hand, Ben O'Grady, Douglas Knight, Matt Kund, TS Pine, Drew Lenhart, LJ Phillips, Christopher Sahloff.

Stories include; Beyond, Grave Threats, Rodwell, Empty Spaces, In The Light of Darkness, Gritt Grimstone, Circuits of a Soul, Visiting Hours, Help Wanted.
