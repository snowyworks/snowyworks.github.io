Enjoying his retirement, Jerry looked forward to a relaxing and happy life after his police career. However, after his wife died, he learned of his terminal diagnosis. To top it all off, his step-children dumped him in an assisted living facility. Now with death quickly approaching, a mysterious doctor visits promising a cure... This book contains the original story, sketches, concept artwork, and a bonus story!

Story & Letters: Drew D. Lenhart
Pencils & Inks: Louie Escauriaga
Colors: Jerry Lucas

Released - January 22, 2025
