When a hail of mysterious objects from space change the course of history, how will the human race cope with such a force? Acceptance or destruction? Imagine these objects, referred to as "Stones", being riddled all over the planet, in every city, town, or field. How would society react? What would people do? A five part book that depicts humanity's acceptance and struggle with the objects as they are present throughout several hundred years of Earth's time.

Story & Letters: Drew D. Lenhart
Illustrations: Alberto Pessoa

Released - December 10, 2025
